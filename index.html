FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What services does Able-Assist provide?

We offer personalised support for individuals with disabilities and aged care clients. This includes help with daily living, transport, companionship, community access, and in-home care under NDIS and aged care programs.

Can you help with NDIS plans and paperwork?

Absolutely! Our team can guide you through the NDIS process—from understanding your plan to helping you manage budgets and services without the stress.

Do I get the same support worker each visit?

Wherever possible, yes. We prioritise continuity so you can build trust with a familiar face who understands your needs and preferences.

Are your carers qualified and background checked?

Yes. Every Able-Assist team member has relevant training, First Aid certification, police checks, and a genuine passion for care.

Do you offer support outside regular business hours?

We provide flexible support options, including evenings, weekends, and even overnight care—tailored to your lifestyle.

How do I get started?

Simply contact us for a free consultation, and we’ll walk you through your options with no obligation.
